Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

Description

This is a recipe for making a peanut butter and jelly sandwich.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Grape Jam (Jelly sucks)

Steps

  1. Using a butter knife, spread peanut butter on a slice of bread.
  2. Spread jam on another slice of bread.
  3. Put pieces of bread together.
